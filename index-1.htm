Laboratory K - group that help to produce science movies, guiding and organizing cinema and telemovie scenes in Europe, Asia, Russia and far East. Our clients also are TV and cinema organisations - Euronews, Canada Television, russian ORT, CTC and TV3 TV channels.



Group organize expeditions in Asia and Africa.

Besides the main specialization of the NGO "Laboratory K" organized and continues to conduct such research expedition as Lake Nong Kai, Thailand, underwater research with the University of Alexandria, 7 th wonder of the world Faroese lighthouse, search expedition geoglyphs in Tunisia and Algeria, the biological study of the underground lake China, attended the expedition to search for Altai and Vitim cosmic body, the phenomenon of fireballs Thai lake Nong Kai, a Russian lake Brosnan, underwater research in Taiwan, underwater sites for archaeological work in the Krasnodar region and Ukraine. The Group has initiated, and conducts searches for the bells, church-owned Russian Pavlo Posad district (the abbot Dionysius).

Group deals with analytics in predictions and by its own method and experience.
